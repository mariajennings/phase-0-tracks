Elephants!

Here is a page in which you will find some introductory information regarding elephants.

You should equip yourself with basic elephant-related knowledge before approahcing one in the wild. You don't want them to think you haven't done your research!

Some things you should know about elephants:

  • Elephants can live for over 70 years.
  • They have the largest brains of any animals.
  • Their gestration period lasts from anywhere between 18-22 months.

Did you know that there are three species of elephants?

  1. The African bush elephant - Loxodonta africana
  2. The African forest elephant - Loxodonta cyclotis
  3. The Asian elephant - Elephas maximus
